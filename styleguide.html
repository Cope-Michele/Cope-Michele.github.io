Site Purpose

This site is dedicated to me! It serves as an online version of my portfolio and also a way for those who have never met me to learn a little bit about who I am and why I am the designer and artist that I am.

Target Audience

Color Palette

Because this site is designed to display my design work and fine art, I wanted to stick with a color scheme that would not overpower or clash with my work. I chose a black and white scheme with a pop color for this purpose.
